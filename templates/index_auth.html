Healthy Web App User Guide

Welcome to Healthy-Bot, your virtual health assistant! Follow these simple steps to start using the web app:

  1. Access the Chat Interface:
    • Click on the icon located at the bottom right corner of the screen to open the chat interface.
  2. Initiate the Conversation:
    • Once the chat interface is open, start the conversation by typing your message or providing information about your symptoms.
  3. Disease Prediction:
    • Medi-Bot can predict diseases based on the symptoms you provide. Describe your symptoms, and Medi-Bot will analyze the information to suggest potential diseases.
  4. Symptoms Information:
    • If you have a specific disease in mind, ask Medi-Bot about the symptoms related to that disease. It will provide you with detailed information to help you understand better.
  5. Find Nearby Hospitals:
    • Medi-Bot can suggest nearby hospital locations based on the provided disease. If you're looking for healthcare facilities related to a specific condition, just ask, and Medi-Bot will guide you to the nearest hospitals.
  6. Enjoy the Conversation:
    • Feel free to ask any health-related questions or share your concerns. Medi-Bot is here to provide information, guidance, and support.
